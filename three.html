Chills
Loss of Taste or Smell
Fever
Muscle Pain
Sore Throat
Cough
Confusion
Rapid Heartbeat
Vomiting
Red Eyes
Diarrhea
Emergency Warning Signs
Persistent Chest Pain or Pressure
